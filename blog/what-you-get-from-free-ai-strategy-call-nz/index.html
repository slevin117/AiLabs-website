Getting started

What you'll get from a free 30-minute AI strategy call

Most NZ business owners think AI is a six-month project. In 30 minutes, we'll tell you where it fits in your business, what it'd actually cost, and whether now is the right time to move on it.

2 May 2026 4 min read

The most common thing we hear after a discovery call: "I didn't realise it could be that straightforward."

Most NZ business owners carry a mental picture of AI that's somewhere between a multi-year IT project and a magic solution they haven't figured out yet. Neither is usually accurate. The actual answer — where AI helps, what it costs, whether the timing is right — is almost always clearer than people expect. You just need 30 minutes with someone who's done it before.

Here's what that looks like.

What we cover in 30 minutes

  • Where AI would make a real difference in your business — specifically. Not "AI can help with customer service" — but "the after-hours enquiries coming in via your website contact form are the highest-value thing to automate, and here's why."
  • What it would realistically take to set up. Time, cost, effort, and what you'd need to have in place first. No surprises later.
  • Whether the timing is right. Some businesses are ready to move. Others have one thing to fix first. We'll tell you which category you're in — honestly.
  • A clear first step. Even if you don't work with us, you'll leave with something actionable: the one thing worth doing first, and a rough sense of what it looks like.

Who the call is for

NZ business owners and operators who are curious about AI but haven't pulled the trigger. You might be:

  • Wondering if the timing is right for your business
  • Unsure what AI would actually do for you (vs. the hype)
  • Interested but not sure where to start or what to budget
  • Skeptical but open — you want the honest picture, not a pitch

You don't need to be technical. You don't need to have a budget in mind. You don't need to be ready to commit to anything.

Who the call is not for

If you're looking to automate everything at once, or you want us to sell you on AI as a concept, this isn't the right starting point. We work best with businesses that have a specific problem they want to solve — even if they're not quite sure what the solution looks like yet.

If you're at the very beginning — still trying to figure out whether AI is relevant to your industry at all — where to start with AI if you run a small NZ business is a better first read. Then come back for the call.

Why it's free

We offer free discovery calls because the businesses that are the best fit for what we do are the ones that go in with clear expectations. A 30-minute conversation is the fastest way to find out if there's a real fit — for both of us. If there isn't, we'll say so.

What you'll leave with

At minimum: clarity. You'll know whether AI is worth pursuing right now, and if so, where to start.

If there's a strong fit: a proposed scope — what we'd build, what it'd cost, and what the expected return would be. No obligation to proceed.

If the timing isn't right: a concrete recommendation for what to do first, and an honest steer on when to revisit.

Common questions

Is there any obligation after the call?

No. The discovery call is genuinely free and obligation-free. We'll give you our honest assessment — including if the answer is "not yet" or "you don't actually need us for this." If there is a fit and you want to move forward, we'll outline what that looks like. But there's no pressure and no sales pitch.

Do I need to prepare anything before the call?

No preparation needed. It helps to have a rough sense of the biggest time sinks in your business — the tasks that feel repetitive or that your team does manually every week. But even if you're not sure, that's what the call is for. We'll ask the right questions.

Who is the call for?

NZ business owners and operators who are curious about AI but aren't sure where to start, what it'd cost, or whether it's right for their stage. You don't need to be technical, you don't need to have a budget in mind, and you don't need to be ready to commit to anything.

30 minutes. No obligation.
